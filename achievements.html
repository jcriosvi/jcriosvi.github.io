Achievements

Achivements on my career

Academic and labor achievements.

  • "Project Management Professional Certification from international PMI organization."

    Getting this certification is the most important achievements in my professional career. It has allowed me to structure work on many fronts of technology.

  • "Leadership in strategic projects supported by the Corporate PMO."

    Projects of all kinds have been led, among the most prominent are:
    -Changes in the ERP. With software validation processes and change control supported by ITSM.
    -Electronic Invoicing Systems of several Latin American countries.
    -Business strategies to increase revenue through direct solutions to customers, such as eLearning strategies.
    -Project to launch new products that include latest generation medical products.

  • "LEAN/Six Sigma Certification."

    Use of LEAN/Six Sigma continuous improvement methodologies to solve business problems. Particularly two time improvement projects using DMAIC and Kaizen Events respectively.

  • "CSM Certified Scrum Master Certification"

    The knowledge was obtained to apply it in a large number of projects that required to be managed in an agile way.

  • "Leadership in mobile application development projects."

    From the portfolio of mobile solutions projects, 3 projects were led with SCRUM methodology for the development of mobile applications.
    -The first project consisted of a solution for direct clients for the consultation of a medical technology.
    -The second was a mobile solution that calculates the status of a particular disease.
    -And the third, a mobile solution to support the work of doctors.
    Software architecture was evaluated and aligned with the company's standard technologies.

  • "Leadership in medium to high complexity projects."

    Projects were led with the following characteristics that make them complex:
    -Number of stakeholders of around 80 people or more.
    -Management of different geographical areas (LATAM, EMEA, APAC).
    -Use of a large amount of support resources to replace the "Face to Face" in projects that are required to do so.
    -Projects with investment budgets greater than USD 500K.

  • "Development of components and artifacts to support specific business processes on the Java platform."

    Development initiatives were led to support:
    -Payroll systems.
    -Customer Collection management systems.
    -Electronic invoicing systems where the fiscal entity requires changes with very short windows of time.

  • "Use of agile methodologies supported by platforms."

    Projects were led with the use of SCRUM supported by the JIRA platform, where the entire cycle was managed to obtain product increments. The handling of the deployment streams was executed with Bitbucket. Execution carried out with geographically dispersed people and teams.

  • "Assessment of solution architecture in projects"

    The solution architecture evaluation was carried out in multiple projects in the LATAM region, allowing an alignment with the company's reference architecture.

The projects are named generically so as not to compromise the Knowhow of the solutions developed. It is not possible to give details of them since they constitute intellectual property rights of my employers. All these achievements have had something in particular, the practical application of the knowledge acquired in the academy.
